CorpersWelfareManager...... Managing Corpers Affair and Funds in local government Area

This Software"CorperWelfareManager" is a software

that Stores Accurate Information of all Corpers in Abuah Oduah local Government area Rivers State

Stores Monthly(N200) Payment of Welfare paid by Corpers and Allocate Financial Resources according to Percentage of paid Corpers Leaving in a Community

What's Inside

Records Corpers Information

Enables Corpers Information to be Recorded in the Database

Records Welfare Payment

Enables Corper Welfare Monthly Payment to be Recorded in the Database

Shares Allocation Resources By 60% to Communities

Allocate Allocation to Corpers Community According to Percentage of Paid Corpers leaving in the Community
